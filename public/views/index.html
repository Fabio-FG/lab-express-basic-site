The origin behind the name

The name Ishiro was first a play on the common Japanese name "Ichirou" which means "first son" but later switched to Ishiro just because of the sound and pronunciation.It is also a play on the word "Ishi" which means "will" or "stone".

Kuroyama is a combination of two elements. Kuro is black which is my favourite colour and Yama means montain which is one of my favourite places
